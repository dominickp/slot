## âš™ï¸ Core Game Mechanics

Le Bandit is a **6x5 grid slot** that uses a **Cluster Pay** system rather than traditional paylines.

### 1. Super Cascades

Whenever you land a winning cluster (5+ matching symbols touching vertically or horizontally), those symbols are removed. However, unlike standard cascades, **all instances** of the winning symbol type are removed from the entire grid, allowing for a massive influx of new symbols.

### 2. Golden Squares and Rainbows

This is the "meat" of the gameplay:

* **Golden Squares:** When a winning cluster is removed, the positions it occupied are highlighted as **Golden Squares**.
* **The Rainbow Symbol:** If a Rainbow symbol lands, it activates all currently highlighted Golden Squares.
* **The Reveal:** Activated squares transform into:
* **Bronze, Silver, or Gold Coins:** Cash prizes ranging from 0.2x to 500x.
* **Four-Leaf Clovers:** Multiplies adjacent coins by up to x10.
* **Pot of Gold:** Collects all coin values on the screen and stores them, then re-activates the squares to reveal new coins.
